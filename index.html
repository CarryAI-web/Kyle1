Valorant Agents Guide

Explore the Agents of Valorant and Their Abilities

What is Valorant?

Valorant is a 5v5 tactical first-person shooter by Riot Games, combining precise gunplay with unique agent abilities. With 27 agents across four roles—Duelists, Initiators, Sentinels, and Controllers—players craft strategic team compositions to dominate matches. Each agent offers distinct skills, from aggressive fragging to defensive utility, shaping the battlefield in ranked and professional play.

Agent Roles

Duelists Initiators Sentinels Controllers

Duelists

Duelists lead aggressive plays, securing kills and creating space for their team.

Jett

  • Cloudburst (C, 200): Throws a smoke cloud to obscure vision.
  • Updraft (Q, 150): Propels Jett upward for high ground.
  • Tailwind (E, Free): Dashes in movement direction (2 kills recharge).
  • Blade Storm (X, 7): Equips deadly throwing knives.

Phoenix

  • Hot Hands (C, 200): Fireball damages enemies, heals Phoenix.
  • Blaze (Q, 150): Fire wall blocks vision, damages others.
  • Curveball (E, 100): Curving flashbang blinds (2 kills recharge).
  • Run it Back (X, 6): Respawns at starting point after death.

Raze

  • Boom Bot (C, 200): Bot chases and explodes on enemies.
  • Blast Pack (Q, 200): Explosive for damage or mobility.
  • Paint Shells (E, Free): Cluster grenade for area damage (1 kill recharge).
  • Showstopper (X, 8): Rocket launcher for massive damage.

Reyna

  • Leer (C, 250): Nearsight orb blinds enemies looking at it.
  • Devour (Q, 200): Consumes soul orb to heal (kill/assist).
  • Dismiss (E, 200): Consumes soul orb for intangibility (kill/assist).
  • Empress (X, 6): Boosts speed, fire rate, and soul orb effects.

Yoru

  • Blindside (C, 250): Flashbang detonates on impact.
  • Gatecrash (Q, 200): Teleport tether for repositioning.
  • Fakeout (E, Free): Decoy mimics footsteps (1 kill recharge).
  • Dimensional Drift (X, 6): Invisible movement, immune to damage.

Neon

  • Relay Bolt (C, 200): Concussing bolt bounces off walls.
  • Fast Lane (Q, 300): Creates two vision-blocking walls.
  • High Gear (E, Free): Sprint with slide (2 kills recharge).
  • Overdrive (X, 7): High-speed mode with electric weapon.

Iso

  • Contingency (C, 250): Deploys a prismatic energy wall.
  • Undercut (Q, 200): Bolt applies vulnerable status.
  • Double Tap (E, Free): Buff for orb-collecting damage boost (2 kills recharge).
  • Kill Contract (X, 7): 1v1 arena against an enemy.

Waylay

  • Ambush (C, 200): Trap slows and reveals enemies.
  • Backstab (Q, 250): Throwable knife with teleport on hit.
  • Feint (E, Free): Decoy distracts enemies (1 kill recharge).
  • Assassin’s Edge (X, 7): Stealth mode with enhanced knife.

Initiators

Initiators gather intel and disrupt enemies to set up team pushes.

Sova

  • Shock Bolt (C, 150): Arrow deals damage in an area.
  • Recon Bolt (Q, 200): Arrow reveals enemies in range.
  • Owl Drone (E, Free): Drone tags enemies (1 kill recharge).
  • Hunter’s Fury (X, 8): Long-range energy blasts.

Breach

  • Aftershock (C, 200): Wall burst damages enemies.
  • Flashpoint (Q, 250): Blinding flash through walls.
  • Fault Line (E, Free): Concussing seismic blast (1 kill recharge).
  • Rolling Thunder (X, 8): Massive concussing shockwave.

Skye

  • Trailblazer (C, 200): Tiger concusses enemies.
  • Guiding Light (Q, 250): Flash hawk blinds enemies.
  • Regrowth (E, Free): Heals allies in range (1 kill recharge).
  • Seekers (X, 7): Tracks and nearsights enemies.

KAY/O

  • FRAG/ment (C, 200): Explosive fragment sticks to ground.
  • FLASH/drive (Q, 250): Throwable flashbang.
  • ZERO/point (E, Free): Blade suppresses enemies (1 kill recharge).
  • NULL/cmd (X, 8): Suppresses enemies, boosts KAY/O.

Fade

  • Seize (C, 200): Orb traps and decays enemies.
  • Prowler (Q, 250): Creature tracks and nearsights enemies.
  • Haunt (E, Free): Reveals enemies in line of sight (1 kill recharge).
  • Nightfall (X, 8): Wave decays and deafens enemies.

Gekko

  • Mosh Pit (C, 200): Explosive pit damages enemies.
  • Wingman (Q, 300): Creature plants/defuses spike or concusses.
  • Dizzy (E, Free): Blinds enemies in line of sight (1 kill recharge).
  • Thrash (X, 7): Remote-controlled creature stuns enemies.

Tejo

  • Flamepoint (C, 200): Molotov damages and slows enemies.
  • Guided Salvo (Q, 250): Fireballs track enemies.
  • Blaze Dash (E, Free): Fiery dash with damage trail (1 kill recharge).
  • Inferno Barrage (X, 8): Multiple explosive fireballs.

Sentinels

Sentinels lock down areas and provide defensive utility for the team.

Sage

  • Barrier Orb (C, 400): Creates a solid wall.
  • Slow Orb (Q, 200): Slows enemies in an area.
  • Healing Orb (E, Free): Heals an ally or self (cooldown).
  • Resurrection (X, 7): Revives a dead ally.

Cypher

  • Trapwire (C, 200): Trap tethers and reveals enemies.
  • Cyber Cage (Q, 100): Cage slows enemies passing through.
  • Spycam (E, Free): Camera tags enemies (cooldown).
  • Neural Theft (X, 6): Reveals enemy locations.

Killjoy

  • Nanoswarm (C, 200): Grenade damages enemies in range.
  • Alarmbot (Q, 200): Bot applies vulnerable to enemies.
  • Turret (E, Free): Shoots enemies in range (cooldown).
  • Lockdown (X, 8): Detains enemies in a large radius.

Chamber

  • Trademark (C, 200): Trap slows and reveals enemies.
  • Headhunter (Q, 100/bullet): Equips a heavy pistol.
  • Rendezvous (E, Free): Two teleport anchors (cooldown).
  • Tour De Force (X, 8): Sniper rifle with slow field on kill.

Deadlock

  • Sonic Sensor (C, 200): Trap concusses nearby enemies.
  • Barrier Mesh (Q, 300): Wall blocks movement.
  • GravNet (E, Free): Net forces enemies to crouch (1 kill recharge).
  • Annihilation (X, 8): Nanowires cocoon an enemy.

Vyse

  • Arc Rose (C, 200): Blinding trap on surfaces, redeployable.
  • Shear (Q, 200): Wall trap blocks bullets.
  • Razorvine (E, Free): Slows and damages enemies (1 kill recharge).
  • Steel Garden (X, 8): Metal spikes disable enemy weapons.

Controllers

Controllers block vision and control map areas to support team strategy.

Brimstone

  • Incendiary (C, 250): Molotov damages enemies in area.
  • Sky Smoke (Q, 100): Deploys up to three smokes.
  • Stim Beacon (E, Free): Boosts fire rate for allies (cooldown).
  • Orbital Strike (X, 7): Laser damages in a large area.

Viper

  • Poison Cloud (C, 200): Gas emitter blocks vision, decays enemies.
  • Toxic Screen (Q, 200): Long gas wall blocks vision, decays.
  • Snake Bite (E, Free): Acid pool damages and slows (2 kills recharge).
  • Viper’s Pit (X, 8): Toxic cloud highlights enemies to Viper.

Omen

  • Shrouded Step (C, 150): Short-range teleport.
  • Paranoia (Q, 250): Blinding projectile through walls.
  • Dark Cover (E, Free): Ascendancy (X, 7): Map-wide teleport with invulnerability.

Astra

  • Gravity Well (C, 150): Pulls enemies to center, explodes.
  • Nova Pulse (Q, 200): Concusses enemies in an area.
  • Nebula/Dissipate (E, Free): Smoke or star recall (1 kill recharge).
  • Astral Form (X, 8): Place stars for abilities.

Harbor

  • Cove (C, 350): Shielded smoke blocks bullets.
  • High Tide (Q, 250): Water wall blocks vision.
  • Cascade (E, Free): Slow-moving water wall (1 kill recharge).
  • Reckoning (X, 7): Geyser concusses enemies in area.

Clove

  • Ruse (C, 200): Smokes block vision, usable post-death.
  • Meddle (Q, 250): Decays and marks enemies.
  • Pick-me-up (E, Free): Speed/health boost from kills/assists.
  • Not Dead Yet (X, 7): Self-resurrection after death.

Tips for Playing Valorant

  • Choose agents that match your playstyle (e.g., Jett for mobility, Sage for support).
  • Coordinate with your team to combine abilities effectively.
  • Practice aim and map knowledge to maximize agent utility.
  • Use Spike Rush or Unrated to test new agents and strategies.
