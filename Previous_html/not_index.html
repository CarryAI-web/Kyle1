Junk Foods and Potential Cancer Risks

  • Processed Meats (e.g., hot dogs, salami, sausage, ham, corned beef, beef jerky) - Associated with colorectal, stomach, and breast cancer.
  • Fried Foods (e.g., french fries, potato chips) - Linked to increased overall cancer risk.
  • Dairy (e.g., milk, cheese, yogurt) - Possibly linked to increased risk of prostate cancer.
  • Sugary Foods and Refined Carbs (e.g., sugar-sweetened beverages, baked goods, white pasta, white bread, white rice, sugary cereals) - Indirectly increases cancer risk by promoting obesity and type 2 diabetes.
  • Packaged Bakery Products - Linked to increased overall cancer risk.
  • Snacks - Linked to increased overall cancer risk.
  • Fizzy Drinks - Linked to increased overall cancer risk.
